Rock . . . Paper . . . Scissors . . . Duel!

Go to the Play page to be matched against a random opponent. Play a friend by setting up a Challenge, or Join a friend's Challenge.

Rules

After two players have joined the game, the count-off will begin: "Rock...Paper...Scissors...Shoot!" Lock in your throw before "Shoot!" appears.

If a throw is not locked in before the end of the count, the player has a No-Throw which can be beaten by any other throw.

Valid throws are scored as:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

Points

The game winner is decided by the best out of 3 Matches. Each Match is determined by the best of 3 Throws.
